About Us

Get to know

LANCE EVENTS was established in 2005 by real estate entrepreneur Donatus Enekwene, who has a passion for creating beautiful spaces with the idea of bringing people together, whether in business, celebration or for the arts. Since its opening, EVENUE has hosted countless events from Meetings & Conferences to Product Launches to Screenings to Private Celebrations and has gained a reputation for its commitment to excellence, outstanding service and uncompromising quality. From sophisticated intimate celebrations to elegant affairs, LANCE EVENTS is dedicated to facilitating exceptional events.
